# Role Guide: Product Manager (Test-only)

## Core Responsibilities
- Define product goals and success metrics
- Write requirements and coordinate delivery with engineering/design
- Manage product backlog and roadmap
- Run discovery (user feedback, support insights)

## First 30 Days Expectations
- Understand current product strategy and roadmap
- Write one PRD and run one stakeholder review
- Define baseline metrics for one feature area

## Common Tools
- Project tracking board
- Product analytics
- Documentation/wiki
- Customer feedback channels

## Who to Ask
- Engineering lead for feasibility
- Design for UX flow and research
- Support lead for recurring customer issues
