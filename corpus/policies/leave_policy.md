# Leave Policy (Test-only)

## Leave Types
- Paid Time Off (PTO): 18 days per year (pro-rated).
- Sick Leave: 10 days per year.
- Casual Leave: 6 days per year.
- Optional Holidays: choose 2 from the optional holiday list.

## Requesting Leave
- Apply through the HR portal at least 7 days in advance for planned leave.
- For emergencies, notify your manager and apply in the portal within 24 hours.

## Approval
- Manager approval required for PTO and Casual Leave.
- Sick leave over 2 consecutive days requires a medical note.

## Carry Forward
- Up to 5 PTO days may be carried forward to the next year.
